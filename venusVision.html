20 years later

Fast fashion is a thing of the past, as the industry transforms itself with a new, ethical approach to clothing manufacturing. Instead of harmful materials, clothes are made from plant-based fiber and fruit waste fiber, reducing the amount of carbon dioxide produced and increasing the amount of oxygen generated.








AR simulation Venus Capsule



But there's a catch - clothes only come in black and white . To overcome this, the fashion world unleashes the power of AR Venus capsules that allow us to change the way we see color forever. With the Venus vision bluetooth connected to the Venus app, users can simulate different colors on their clothes and even find out the sustainability of the clothing others are wearing.

Venus App

The Venus app is designed to be bluetooth to the Venus capsule. After consuming the capsule allows up to 5 minutes for the activation to happen in your vision. This app is designed for Venus consumers to see colors of the clothing items that is simulate through their AR eyes.
