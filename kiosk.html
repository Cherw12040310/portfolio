About the Project

The MTA installed interactive kiosks on train platforms so riders can look up service information while waiting for their trains. The screens also show ads and public service announcements every few minutes. This is a display of my intuitive, easy-to-use interface for subway passengers to look up their horoscopes in the kiosk
